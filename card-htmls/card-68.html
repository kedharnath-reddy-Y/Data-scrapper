AU Bank LIT Credit Card Review

The AU Bank LIT Credit Card is an excellent choice for people who want a credit card that can be customized to their liking, with plenty of rewards and benefits. This fantastic card offers a range of benefits, including cashback, rewards, travel perks, purchase protection, and no annual fee. With the ability to personalize the benefits according to personal preferences, the AU Bank LIT Credit Card sets itself apart from other credit cards, giving individuals the freedom to choose what suits their lifestyle and needs.
Whether you prefer cashback rewards, travel benefits, or purchase protection, this card caters to your unique needs. However, it is essential to evaluate your requirements and the associated fees before applying for this card. Overall, the AU Bank LIT Credit Card provides great value for money.

Who should get the AU Bank LIT Credit Card?

The AU Bank LIT Credit Card is ideal for individuals seeking a luxurious and rewarding shopping experience. It offers 5X/10X Reward Points on retail transactions, along with milestone and category cashback options. With airport lounge access and memberships to entertainment and fitness platforms, it caters to frequent travellers and those who value convenience. The card also provides security with card liability cover and credit shield. Overall, the AU Bank LIT Credit Card is perfect for those who prioritize luxury, rewards, and exclusive privileges in their banking experience.

How to apply for AU Bank LIT Credit Card?

There are two ways to apply for the LIT Credit Card: online and offline.

Online application

  • Download the AU 0101 app on your phone.
  • Log in and go to the credit card homepage and click on "Manage your credit card".
  • Check the price of each feature and choose as per your affordability.
  • You can choose or modify your features as per your needs.
  • You can also switch off your auto-renewal whenever you need it on your app.
  • You can keep track of your spending and manage it on the AU 0101 app.

Offline application

  • Visit the nearest AU Bank branch.
  • Fill in the credit card application form and attach the documents required.

AU Bank LIT Credit Card Eligibility

  • Age: 21 to 60 years.
  • Resident: Indian.
  • Income: Applicants must have a stable source of income to be eligible for this credit card.

Documents Required

  • Proof of Identity: PAN Card, Aadhaar card, Driver’s License, Passport, Voter’s ID, Overseas Citizen of India Card, Person of Indian Origin Card, Job card issued by NREGA, Letters issued by the UIDAI or any other government-approved photo ID proof.
  • Proof of Address: Aadhaar card, Driver’s License, Passport, Utility Bill not more than 3 months old, Ration Card, Property Registration Document, Person of Indian Origin Card, Job card issued by NREGA, Bank Account Statement, or any other government-approved address proof.
  • Proof of Income: Latest one or two salary slips (not more than three months old), latest Form 16, and the last 3 months’ bank statement.

Frequently Asked Questions
1. When can I redeem earned reward points through AU Small Finance Bank LIT Credit Card?

You can redeem the reward points earned through AU Small Finance Bank LIT Credit Card post the statement is generated.

2. What are the benefits of the LIT Credit Card?

The LIT Credit Card offers a number of benefits, including:

  • No annual fee
  • High rewards: You can earn up to 5% cashback on online shopping and dining.
  • Customizable features: You can choose the features that are most important to you, such as travel insurance, cashback, or rewards points.
3. What are the eligibility criteria for the LIT Credit Card?

To be eligible for the LIT Credit Card, you must:

  • Be a resident of India.
  • Have a stable source of income.
  • Have a good credit score.
4. What is the interest-free period for AU Small Finance Bank LIT Credit Card?

The interest-free period for AU Small Finance Bank LIT Credit Card is up to 48 days (not applicable for cash withdrawal).

5. How do I apply for the LIT Credit Card?

You can apply for the LIT Credit Card online or offline. To apply online, you can download the AU 0101 app on your phone and follow the instructions. To apply offline, you can visit the nearest AU Bank branch and fill in the application form.

6. How can I build my LIT Credit Card?

To build your LIT Credit Card, you must follow the steps mentioned below:

Step 1: Visit the official website of AU Bank & scan the barcode to download AU 0101 app
Step 2: Go to the credit card homepage & click on ‘Manage Credit Card’ to explore the available benefits
Step 3: Check the pricing of each feature & select the one that suits your needs the best
Step 4: Now, choose the feature. You can disable the auto-renewal of any feature through AU 0101 App
Step 5: You can now manage and track your cashback, and rewards from existing active benefits
