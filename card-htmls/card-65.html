American Express Gold Credit Card Review

If you are a frequent traveller or someone who spends a lot on dining and other eligible categories, then the American Express Gold Credit Card could be an ideal option for you. This credit card comes packed with several benefits that cater to your lifestyle needs. You can earn decent rewards, get exclusive benefits, and enjoy 24/7 customer support. 

One of the most unique features of this card is that it offers no pre-set credit limit, so you can spend freely without worrying about hitting a limit. Additionally, the card lets you earn a reward rate as high as 6%, which means you can accumulate reward points quickly. You can even earn reward points on your fuel and utility expenses which are often excluded from other reward programs.

However, it's worth noting that the American Express Gold Credit Card comes with a high annual fee and limited acceptance. So, while it may be a good fit for some users, it may not be suitable for everyone.

Who should get the American Express Gold Credit Card?

The American Express Gold Credit Card is best suited for individuals who usually spend a lot on travel, daily utilities, shopping, dining, etc., and also wish to earn rewards on all the spending done across these categories in the form of reward points. Users who wish to have all these premium benefits by paying a fee of ₹4,500 may find this card best suited for themselves on a day-to-day basis.

How to apply for an American Express Gold Credit Card?

American Express Gold Credit Cards can be applied by calling 1-800-419-2122 and through an online application process as well. To apply online, follow the below-mentioned steps:

  • Visit the American Express Website. 

  • The "Gold Credit Card" is listed under the "Card” section.

  • Click on "Apply Now" and start the application process.

  • Fill in all the required details and upload all the necessary documents to complete the process.

American Express Gold Credit Card Eligibility

  • Age: 18 years and above
  • Income Required: ₹6 lakh per annum
  • Have a good credit history and no payment defaults
     

Documents Required

  • Proof of Identity: PAN Card, Aadhaar card, Driver’s License, Passport, Voter’s ID, Overseas Citizen of India Card, Person of Indian Origin Card, Job card issued by NREGA, Letters issued by the UIDAI or any other government-approved photo ID proof.
  • Proof of Address: Aadhaar card, Driver’s License, Passport, Utility Bill not more than 3 months old, Ration Card, Property Registration Document, Person of Indian Origin Card, Job card issued by NREGA, Bank Account Statement, or any other government-approved address proof.
  • Proof of Income: Latest one or two salary slips (not more than 3 months old), latest Form 16, and the last 3 months’ bank statement.

Frequently Asked Questions
1. What is the limit on the American Express Gold Credit Card?

This is a charge card with no pre-set limit, which means you can spend as much as you want. Your transactions are approved based on your financial situation, credit history, spending habits, and account history.

2. What is American Express Gold credit card interest rate?

The American Express Gold Card has a variable APR (annual percentage rate) of 15.49% to 25.49%. 

3. Can I withdraw cash using my American Express Gold Credit Card?

Yes, you can withdraw cash from any eligible ATM with your American Express Credit Card. At an ATM, you can get a cash advance using your Amex Gold card. Insert the card into the ATM, enter your PIN, and select the cash advance option.

4. Is American Express Gold Credit Card suitable for travel benefits?

Yes, the American Express Gold Card is suitable for travel benefits as it offers complimentary room upgrades and hotel credits on stays at top hotels, including Hilton Hotels, Intercontinental Hotels Group, and Hyatt Hotels.

5. What is the credit limit offered on American Express Gold Card?

Because the American Express Gold Credit Card does not have a set spending limit, the available credit is determined each month based on the cardholder's previous spending habits, income, payment history, and other factors. This does not imply that customers have no spending limits and can spend as much as they want. Instead of a fixed spending limit, Amex Gold Credit Cardholders receive a flexible spending allowance.

6. Who is eligible for Amex Gold?

A rewarding experience is provided by the American Express Gold Credit Card, which also comes with an unlimited credit limit, cash back, and travel benefits. You must be at least 18 years old and make at least Rs. 6 lahks annually to be eligible. Additionally, you must have a current or savings account with an Indian or international bank and a solid credit history.
