Who should get the SBI Prime Credit Card?

SBI Prime Credit Card is an ideal choice for people who frequently travel and want some exclusive travel benefits. The card offers a wide range of travel benefits, including premium membership with access to hotels and airline and airport lounge benefits. This card is best suited for people who spend in these categories.

In a nutshell, if you want premium perks and are willing to pay a high annual fee, this card is perfect for you.

1. SBI Card Revises Reward Benefits

SBI Card has announced significant revisions to its credit card rewards program, impacting several popular cards. Key changes include:

  • SimplyCLICK SBI Card: Starting 1 April 2025, reward points for online spending at Swiggy will be reduced from 10X to 5X. However, 10X reward points will continue for online purchases at Apollo 24X7, BookMyShow, Cleartrip, Dominos, IGP, Myntra, Netmeds, and Yatra.

  • Air India SBI Platinum Credit Card: Effective 31 March 2025, reward points for Air India ticket purchases via the airline's website or mobile app will decrease from 15 to 5 points per Rs. 100 spent.

  • Air India SBI Signature Credit Card: Also, from 31 March 2025, reward points for similar Air India ticket purchases will drop from 30 to 10 points per Rs. 100 spent.

Additionally, as of July 26, 2025, the complimentary air accident insurance coverage of Rs. 50 lakh, and rail accident insurance coverage of Rs. 10 lakh currently offered on these cards will be discontinued.

Cardholders are advised to review these changes and adjust their spending strategies accordingly.

Frequently Asked Questions
1. Can I earn a fuel surcharge waiver on every transaction via SBI Prime Credit Card?

No, you can earn a 1% fuel surcharge waiver on transactions ranging from Rs. 500 to Rs. 4,000. However, you can only get a Rs. 250 fuel surcharge waiver per month. This applies to all transactions made at petrol pumps in India.

2. How many add-on credit cards can I avail on my SBI Card PRIME?

A cardholder may obtain up to two SBI add-on credit cards for each primary card. After applying for the add-on card, the cardholder can check the status of the application by following these steps: Log in to the primary cardholder's account using the username and password. The application of the add-on card can then be tracked online. You can also check the status of your application by calling the SBI helpline at 1860-180-1290 or 39-02-02-02. (provide your STD code).

3. How to report the loss of my SBI Card PRIME Credit Card?

If you have misplaced your SBI Card PRIME Credit Card, please immediately contact the SBI Card PRIME customer service team at 1800 180 1290. (toll-free). The customer service team will assist you in blocking the card and obtaining a replacement.

4. Can I use SBI Card PRIME abroad?

Yes, the cardholder can use it anywhere across the globe. The SBI Card Prime is accepted at over 24 million locations worldwide. Every time the cardholder uses the card abroad, a foreign transaction fee of up to 3.5% of the transaction amount will be charged.

5. What is the SBI Credit Card customer care number?

The SBI Credit Card customer care number is 1800 180 1290. The SBI Credit Card customer care number is a 24/7 helpline which provides assistance to customers for any queries related to the credit car.

6. Is a balance transfer facility available on SBI Card PRIME?

Yes, the SBI Card Prime credit card includes a balance transfer on EMI feature that allows cardholders to transfer the outstanding balance on their other bank credit cards to the SBI Card Prime credit card. Once the funds are transferred, cardholders can repay them in easy monthly installments at a lower interest rate with flexible repayment terms.

7. Who is eligible for an SBI prime card?

You must be at least 18 years old, have a valid Indian address, and earn at least Rs. 6 lakhs per year to be eligible for an SBI Prime Card. A good credit score and a track record of repayment are also required. To apply, fill out a simple application form and submit documents such as proof of address, identification, the most recent pay stubs, and bank statements for the previous six months. Once you have submitted all of the required documents, your application will be processed.
