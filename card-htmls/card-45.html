Myntra Kotak Credit Card Review

The Myntra Kotak Credit Card is a great choice for frequent Myntra shoppers who want to save money. With a discount of 7.5%, this card offers even more savings than the Flipkart Axis Credit Card, which only provides 5% cashback on Myntra purchases. Additionally, this credit card includes access to domestic lounges, which is a great perk for those who enjoy travelling within the country. For an annual fee of ₹500, this card provides reasonable cashback rewards and other benefits.

Who Should Opt for Myntra Kotak Credit Card?

The Myntra Kotak Credit Card is a good option for people who frequently shop on Myntra and want to save money. Here are some of the people who should consider opting for this credit card:

  • People who frequently shop on Myntra: The Myntra Kotak Credit Card offers an instant discount of 7.5% on all purchases made on Myntra. This is a great way to save money on your fashion purchases.
  • People who shop at select partner merchants: The Myntra Kotak Credit Card also offers 5% cashback on spends at select partner merchants, such as Zomato, Swiggy, BookMyShow, and more. If you frequently shop at these merchants, you can save money using this card.

How to apply for Myntra Kotak Credit Card?

To apply for Myntra Kotak Credit Card, follow the below steps:

Step 1: Visit select.finology.in and click on 'Credit Card' tab.
Step 2: Click on Card Issuers, where you will see a list of banks that issue credit cards.
Step 3: Choose Kotak Bank from the list.
Step 4: Choose Myntra Kotak Credit Card and click on 'Apply Now' button.
Step 5: Provide the required consent, and then you will be redirected to a page where you need to fill in your mobile number and PAN card number.
Step 6: Fill in the credit card application form and attach the documents required.

Myntra Kotak Credit Card Eligibility

Age: 18 to 65 years
Income: Applicant must have a stable source of income to be eligible for this card.

Documents Required

  • Proof of Identity: PAN Card, Aadhaar card, Driver’s License, Passport, Voter’s ID, Overseas Citizen of India Card, Person of Indian Origin Card, Job card issued by NREGA, Letters issued by the UIDAI or any other government-approved photo ID proof.
  • Proof of Address: Aadhaar card, Driver’s License, Passport, Utility Bill not more than 3 months old, Ration Card, Property Registration Document, Person of Indian Origin Card, Job card issued by NREGA, Bank Account Statement, or any other government-approved address proof.
  • Proof of Income: Latest one or two salary slips (not more than three months old), latest Form 16, and the last 3 months’ bank statement.

Frequently Asked Questions
1. What are the benefits of having a Myntra Kotak Credit Card?

The Myntra Kotak Credit Card offers a number of benefits, including:

  • Instant discount of 7.5% on all purchases made on Myntra: This is the most attractive benefit of the card. You can avail of this discount on all purchases made on Myntra, including clothes, accessories, home & living products, and more.
  • 5% cashback on spends at select partner merchants: The Myntra Kotak Credit Card also offers 5% cashback on spends at select partner merchants, such as Zomato, Swiggy, BookMyShow, and more. The list of partner merchants is subject to change.
  • 1.25% unlimited cashback on spends across other categories: This cashback is applicable on all other spends, except for rent, fuel, EMI transactions, post-purchase EMI, wallet-loading transactions, and cash advances.
  • Welcome benefit of Myntra voucher worth Rs. 500: You get a Myntra voucher worth Rs. 500 on completing the first transaction worth Rs. 500 or more within 30 days of card issuance.
2. Can I use the Myntra Kotak Credit Card for purchases other than on Myntra?

Yes, you can use the Myntra Kotak Credit Card for purchases other than on Myntra. The card can be used at any merchant that accepts Mastercard or Rupay credit cards. You will still get the 7.5% instant discount on all purchases made on Myntra, but you will only get the 5% cashback on spends at select partner merchants and the 1.25% unlimited cashback on other spends if you make those purchases at the partner merchants or other merchants respectively.

The Myntra Kotak Credit Card also offers a number of other benefits, such as complimentary lounge access, free PVR tickets, and Myntra Insider membership. These benefits are available regardless of where you use the card.

3. What is the Myntra Kotak Credit Card?

The Myntra Kotak Credit Card is a co-branded credit card issued by Kotak Mahindra Bank and Myntra. It offers an instant discount of 7.5% on all purchases made on Myntra. It also offers 5% cashback on spends at select partner merchants, such as Zomato, Swiggy, BookMyShow, and more. Other benefits of the card include complimentary lounge access, free PVR tickets, and Myntra Insider membership. The annual fee of the card is Rs.500.

4. How do I apply for a Myntra Kotak Credit Card?

There are two ways to apply for a Myntra Kotak Credit Card:

  • Online: You can apply for the card online through the Kotak Mahindra Bank website. To do this, you will need to provide your personal and financial information, such as your name, address, income, and employment details. You will also need to upload a copy of your PAN card and recent salary slip.
  • At a Kotak Mahindra Bank branch: You can also apply for the card at any Kotak Mahindra Bank branch. To do this, you will need to fill out an application form and provide the same documents as you would if you were applying online.

Here are the documents that you will need to apply for a Myntra Kotak Credit Card:

  • PAN card
  • Recent salary slip
  • Identity proof (such as a passport or driving license)
  • Address proof (such as a utility bill)
5. How can I track my Myntra Kotak Credit Card application status?

There are a few ways to track your Myntra Kotak Credit Card application status:

  • Online: You can track your application status online through the Kotak Mahindra Bank website. To do this, you will need to create an account and login to your account. Once you are logged in, you will be able to see the status of your application.
  • By SMS: You can also track your application status by SMS. To do this, you will need to register for SMS alerts when you apply for the card. Once your application is processed, you will receive an SMS with the status of your application.
  • By phone: You can also track your application status by phone. To do this, you can call the Kotak Mahindra Bank customer service number and provide your application number. The customer service representative will be able to tell you the status of your application.
6. Is the Myntra Kotak Credit Card accepted internationally?

Yes, the Myntra Kotak Credit Card is accepted internationally. It is a Mastercard, which is accepted at millions of merchants around the world. However, you may be charged a foreign currency transaction fee if you use the card to make purchases in foreign currency. The foreign currency transaction fee is typically around 3.5% plus GST of the transaction amount.
