American Express Platinum Reserve Credit Card Review

The American Express Platinum Reserve Credit Card is a high-end credit card that offers exclusive benefits to its users. It's perfect for those who want to enjoy perks like free stays, vouchers for movies or shopping, access to lounges, golf privileges, free memberships, and much more. This card is particularly suitable for people who frequently shop online or book movie tickets, don't travel daily, and have low fuel requirements. Who should apply for an American Express Platinum Reserve Credit Card?

The American Express Platinum Reserve Credit Card is ideal for:

  • Frequent travellers who value airport lounge access, travel insurance, and hotel status.
  • High spenders who can leverage travel, dining, and entertainment benefits.
  • Individuals seeking luxury perks like airport transfers and concierge services.

How to apply for an American Express Platinum Reserve Credit Card?

To apply for American Express Platinum Reserve Credit Card, follow the below steps:

Step 1: Visit select.finology.in and click on the 'Credit Card' tab.

Step 2: Click on Card Issuers, where you will see a list of banks that issue credit cards.

Step 3: Choose American Express Bank from the list.

Step 4: Choose American Express Platinum Reserve Credit Card and click on the 'Apply Now' button.

Step 5: Provide the required consent, and then you will be redirected to a page where you need to fill in your mobile number and PAN card number.

Step 6: Fill in the credit card application form and attach the documents required.

American Express Platinum Reserve Credit Card Eligibility

  • Age: 18 years and above
  • Income: ₹6 lakh per annum for salaried and self-employed
  • Resident: Indian

Documents Required

  • Proof of Identity: PAN Card, Aadhaar card, Driver’s License, Passport, Voter’s ID, Overseas Citizen of India Card, Person of Indian Origin Card, Job card issued by NREGA, Letters issued by the UIDAI or any other government-approved photo ID proof.
  • Proof of Address: Aadhaar card, Driver’s License, Passport, Utility Bill not more than 3 months old, Ration Card, Property Registration Document, Person of Indian Origin Card, Job card issued by NREGA, Bank Account Statement, or any other government-approved address proof.
  • Proof of Income: Latest one or two salary slips (not more than 3 months old), latest Form 16, and the last 3 months’ bank statement.
