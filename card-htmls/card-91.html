ixigo AU Bank Credit Card Review

The ixigo credit card stands out as a compelling option for frequent domestic travellers, particularly those who regularly use ixigo's platform for their booking needs. The card's partnership with ixigo ensures 10% discounts on flights, hotels, and bus bookings, making travel planning both economical and convenient.

An impressive feature is this card segment's zero forex markup, which significantly reduces the cost of international transactions. Coupled with complimentary lounge access, the card enhances the overall travel experience.

However, the exclusion of categories like fuel, rent, and insurance from earning rewards may limit its appeal for users with significant spending in these areas. And if you don’t use the ixigo platform for booking your travel, then this card might not be as beneficial as it can be otherwise.

Who Should Get an ixigo AU Credit Card?

The ixigo AU Credit Card is ideal for those who:

  • Frequently book through ixigo and can maximise the platform-specific discounts
  • Desire complimentary lounge (airport + railway) access to enhance the travel comfort
  • Make international transactions and can benefit from zero forex markup
  • Can meet the spending thresholds to avail annual fee waiver
  • Frequently travel within India

Note: Existing AU Credit Card holders cannot apply for the ixigo AU Credit Card or any other AU Bank Credit Card.

How to Apply for ixigo AU Credit Card?

To apply online for an ixigo AU Bank Credit Card, follow the below steps:

Step 1: Visit AU Small Finance Bank's website and click on the 'Credit Card' tab
Step 2: Choose ixigo AU Credit Card and click on the 'Apply Now' button
Step 3: Provide the required consent, and then you will be redirected to a page where you need to fill in your mobile number and PAN card number
Step 4: Fill in the credit card application form and attach the documents required

To apply offline for an ixigo AU Bank Credit Card, follow these steps:

  • Visit the nearest AU Small Finance Bank branch
  • Fill in the credit card application form and attach the documents required.

Eligibility Criteria for ixigo AU Credit Card

  • Primary cardholders should be between 21 and 75 years of age
  • Add-on cardholders should be over 18 years
  • Applicant must be a resident of India
  • Applicant should be a user registered on ixigo platforms.

Documents Required

  • Proof of Identity: PAN Card, Aadhaar card, Driver’s License, Passport, Voter’s ID, Overseas Citizen of India Card, Person of Indian Origin Card, Job card issued by NREGA, Letters issued by the UIDAI or any other government-approved photo ID proof.
  • Proof of Address: Aadhaar card, Driver’s License, Passport, Utility Bill not more than 3 months old, Ration Card, Property Registration Document, Person of Indian Origin Card, Job card issued by NREGA, Bank Account Statement, or any other government-approved address proof.
  • Proof of Income: Latest one or two salary slips (not more than three months old), latest Form 16, and the last 3 months’ bank statement.

Frequently Asked Questions
1. Where can I access the lounge with my ixigo AU Credit Card?

For eligible list of lounges, click on the following:

1. International lounges
2. Domestic Lounges
3. Railway Lounges
 

2. Is the AU ixigo Credit Card worth it?

The AU ixigo Credit Card is ideal for frequent travellers, offering benefits like travel rewards, lounge access, and discounts on ixigo bookings. If you travel often and can maximise these perks, the card can be valuable.

3. What is the value of 1 reward point on the AU ixigo Credit Card?

Generally, 1 Reward Point is valued at Rs. 0.25. However, this may vary depending on the specific card variant and the type of rewards being redeemed, such as e-gift vouchers, movies, merchandise, and so on.

4. Is the ixigo AU Credit Card free?

No, the ixigo AU Credit Card comes with an annual fee. However, the fee may be waived if you meet certain spending criteria. Additionally, it is being issued lifetime free for a certain period of time. Check the latest terms with AU Small Finance Bank for details.

5. Does the ixigo AU Credit Card offer lounge access?

Yes, the ixigo AU Credit Card offers access to domestic airports, international airports, and railway lounges. However, credit cardholders must spend a minimum of Rs. 20,000 in a calendar quarter to become eligible for complimentary domestic airport lounge access within the same quarter.
